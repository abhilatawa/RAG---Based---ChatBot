HOW TO SUBMIT AN EXPENSE
1. Log in to Expensify (expenses.finsolve.com)
2. Click "New Expense" and upload receipt
3. Fill in: amount, date, category, project code
4. Add description (required for expenses over $50)
5. Submit to your direct manager for approval
6. Finance processes reimbursements every Friday

Expense Limits (no pre-approval needed):
- Meals (individual):    Up to $30
- Meals (client/team):  Up to $75/person
- Travel (domestic):    Economy class only
- Hotel:                Up to $250/night
- Software/tools:       Up to $100/month (check with IT first)

Expenses above limits require manager + Finance pre-approval.

CODE OF CONDUCT
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment
- Protect confidential company and customer information
- Report concerns to HR (hr@finsolve.com) or anonymously via ethics hotline
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to your manager and HR

IT & SECURITY POLICIES
- Use company-issued devices for all work
- Enable full-disk encryption on all devices
- Use the company VPN when working remotely
- Never share passwords or access credentials
- Report any suspicious activity to security@finsolve.com immediately
- Mandatory security training: complete within 30 days of joining

FinSolve Technologies — HR Policy Handbook 2024
Department: HR | Access: HR, C-Level

LEAVE POLICY

Annual Leave
- All full-time employees receive 25 days paid annual leave per year
- Leave accrues at 2.08 days per month
- Up to 5 days can be carried over to the next calendar year
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via HRPortal

Sick Leave
- 10 days paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not carry over year to year

Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks fully paid
- Adoption: same as parental leave
- Flexible return-to-work options available

Remote Work Policy
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Core hours: 10am–3pm in local timezone
- All remote work must be from a secure, private location
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time setup, $50/month ongoing

PERFORMANCE REVIEW CYCLE
- Mid-year review: July (informal, manager feedback)
- Annual review: December (formal, compensation decisions)
- Probation review: 90 days after start date
- Promotion cycle: Aligned to annual review

COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
- Salary bands reviewed annually in January
- Performance bonus: up to 15% of base salary
- Equity (options): granted at hire, 4-year vest, 1-year cliff
- Health insurance: 100% premium covered (employee), 80% (dependents)
- Dental & Vision: 100% covered
- 401k: 4% company match, immediate vesting
- Learning budget: $2,000 per year per employee
- Wellness budget: $500 per year

ONBOARDING CHECKLIST
Week 1:
  - IT setup: laptop, accounts, access provisioned
  - HR orientation: policies, benefits enrollment
  - Meet your team and manager
  - Complete mandatory compliance training

Week 2-4:
  - Role-specific onboarding with buddy
  - Shadow customer calls (for customer-facing roles)
  - Complete product training modules
  - 30-day check-in with manager

DISCIPLINARY POLICY
